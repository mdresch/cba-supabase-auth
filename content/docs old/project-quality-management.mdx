# Project Quality Management

Project Quality Management is a crucial knowledge area that focuses on incorporating the organization's quality policy regarding planning, managing, and controlling project and product quality requirements. It aims to ensure that the project will satisfy the needs for which it was undertaken and meet stakeholders' expectations.

## Key Processes

1. Plan Quality Management
2. Manage Quality
3. Control Quality

## Input Documents

- Project charter
- Project management plan components
- Enterprise environmental factors
- Organizational process assets
- Stakeholder register
- Risk register
- Requirements documentation
- Quality metrics
- Work performance data
- Approved change requests
- Deliverables
- Project documents

## Output Documents

- Quality management plan
- Quality metrics
- Quality checklists
- Quality control measurements
- Work performance information
- Verified deliverables
- Quality control measurements
- Quality audit reports
- Change requests
- Project management plan updates
- Project documents updates
- Organizational process assets updates

Effective quality management is vital for project success, as it helps ensure that the project deliverables meet the required standards and satisfy stakeholder expectations, ultimately leading to customer satisfaction and project success.
